Most Common FAQs

What is the process of filing an income tax return through WhatsApp?
The process involves sending your tax-related information and documents to our WhatsApp number. Our system will guide you through the required steps and forms to complete your tax return.
Is it secure to file my income tax return through WhatsApp?
Yes, we prioritize the security of your information. We have implemented encryption and data protection measures to ensure the confidentiality of your personal and financial details.
What documents do I need to file my income tax return through WhatsApp?
The specific documents required may vary based on your individual circumstances. Generally, you'll need your PAN (Permanent Account Number), Form 16/16A, bank statements, investment details, and any other relevant income or deduction documents.
How can I get started with filing my income tax return through WhatsApp?
Simply save our WhatsApp number and initiate the conversation. Our system will guide you through the process, requesting necessary information and documents along the way.
Are there any specific requirements for using WhatsApp to file income tax returns?
You will need a smartphone with WhatsApp installed and a stable internet connection to use our WhatsApp filing service.
Can I file income tax returns for someone else through WhatsApp?
Yes, you can file income tax returns for someone else through our WhatsApp service. However, you may need to provide appropriate authorization and supporting documentation.
Is customer support available if I have questions or need assistance with the WhatsApp filing process?
Yes, we provide customer support to address any questions or concerns you may have during the WhatsApp filing process. You can reach out to us through WhatsApp, email, or phone.
Are there any advantages or benefits of filing income tax returns through WhatsApp compared to traditional methods?
Filing through WhatsApp offers convenience, as you can complete the process from the comfort of your smartphone. It saves time and eliminates the need for physical paperwork.
